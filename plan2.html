Toji Workout Plan

🟥 Day 1 (Monday): Chest and Triceps

  • 🔥 Dips (Weighted if possible) – 4 × 6-8 reps
  • 🔥 Incline Dumbbell Bench Press – 4 × 6-8 reps
  • 🔥 Dumbbell Bench Press – 4 × 6-8 reps
  • 🔥 Overhead Dumbbell Triceps Extension – 3 × 10-12 reps
  • 🔥 Triceps Pushdowns (if available) – 3 × 10-12 reps

🟦 Day 2 (Tuesday): Back and Biceps

  • 💪 Pull-Ups – 4 × 6-8 reps
  • 💪 Lat Pulldown – 4 × 8-10 reps
  • 💪 Dumbbell Rows – 4 × 8-10 reps
  • 💪 Barbell Curls (or Dumbbell Curls) – 3 × 10-12 reps
  • 💪 Hammer Curls – 3 × 10-12 reps

🟨 Day 3 (Thursday): Shoulders and Traps

  • ⚡ Overhead Dumbbell Shoulder Press – 4 × 6-8 reps
  • ⚡ Lateral Raises – 3 × 12-15 reps
  • ⚡ Front Dumbbell Raises – 3 × 12-15 reps
  • ⚡ Dumbbell Shrugs – 4 × 8-10 reps
  • ⚡ Rear Delt Flyes – 3 × 10-12 reps

🟩 Day 4 (Friday): Upper Body Push and Pull

  • 🏋️ Dumbbell Bench Press – 4 × 6-8 reps
  • 🏋️ Pull-Ups – 4 × 6-8 reps
  • 🏋️ Dips (Weighted if possible) – 4 × 6-8 reps
  • 🏋️ Overhead Dumbbell Shoulder Press – 3 × 8-10 reps
  • 🏋️ Dumbbell Rows – 4 × 8-10 reps
  • 🏋️ Barbell Curls (or Dumbbell Curls) – 3 × 10-12 reps
