Garou Workout Plan

🟥 Day 1 & 4: Push (Chest, Shoulders, Triceps, Core)

🏃 Jogging – 20 min steady

  • 💥 Explosive Push-ups – 4 × 10-15 reps
  • 💥 Archer Push-ups – 3 × 6-10 reps
  • 💥 Pike Push-ups – 3 × 8-12 reps
  • 💥 Dips (use chairs) – 3 × 8-12 reps
  • 💥 Plank to Push-ups – 3 × 10 reps
  • 💥 Hollow Body Hold – 3 × 30 sec

🟦 Day 2 & 5: Pull (Back, Biceps, Grip, Core)

🏃 Jogging – 20 min steady

  • 🔥 Pull-ups (or Negatives) – 4 × 3-8 reps
  • 🔥 Chin-ups – 3 × 3-8 reps
  • 🔥 Superman Pulls – 3 × 10-12 reps
  • 🔥 Hanging Knee Raises – 3 × 10 reps
  • 🔥 Reverse Snow Angels – 3 × 10 reps
  • 🔥 Leg Raises – 3 × 8-12 reps
  • 🔥 Russian Twists – 3 × 20 reps

🟩 Day 3: Legs (Quads, Hamstrings, Glutes, Explosiveness)

🏃 Sprint Intervals – 30 sec sprint → 90 sec jog (Repeat 5-6 times)

  • ⚡ Jump Squats – 4 × 10 reps
  • ⚡ Bulgarian Split Squats – 3 × 8 reps per leg
  • ⚡ Pistol Squats (Assist if Needed) – 3 × 6 reps per leg
  • ⚡ Wall Sit – 3 × 30-60 sec
  • ⚡ Calf Raises – 3 × 20 reps
  • ⚡ Hanging Knee Raises – 3 × 8-12 reps
